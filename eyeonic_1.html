What to Look for When Buying Glasses

  • Fit and Comfort: Ensure the frame fits your face shape and sits comfortably without pressure points.
  • Lens Quality: Opt for high-quality lenses with UV protection (for sunglasses) or anti-reflective coatings (for eyeglasses).
  • Prescription Accuracy: For contact lenses and prescription glasses, verify the prescription with an eye care professional.
  • Durability: Choose materials like titanium or acetate for long-lasting wear.
  • Style: Select a design that complements your personal style and lifestyle.
